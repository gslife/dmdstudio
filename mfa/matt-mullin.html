Artist Biography

Matthew Mullin is a New England-based designer with six years of industry experience. He received a BA in 2014 from Bridgewater State University, majoring in Graphic Design and minoring in Art History, and is currently pursuing an MFA at the University of Connecticut in Digital Media & Design.

Artist Statement

His current work is rooted in UX/UI design, where he aims to create visually engaging user interfaces. His goal is to implement UX/UI practices seamlessly to create intuitive interfaces that nurture a more intimate experience for the user.
