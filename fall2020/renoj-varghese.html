Artist Biography

Renoj Varghese is an experience and product designer. He is a Connecticut native but was born in Kuwait in 1997. He graduated from the University of Connecticut in 2019 with a Bachelor of Arts in Digital Media and Design as well as Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. Currently Renoj is pursuing a Master of Fine Arts at the University of Connecticut with a focus on Web and Interaction Design. He has a strong interest in digital products and how the smaller details of interaction design and visual design can contribute to the overall process of solving a core problem.

Artist Statement

I come to my work with a technical mind and creative heart. I rely on my experiences in user research and computer programming to give an analytical approach to my design. Additionally, I never forget that I design for people. All design is about making things for others. There's a responsibility to bring a user's perspective when designing for them.

One area of interest is into microinteractions:. They are the effects and reactions from an interface when a user makes an action. It could be a simple animation or overall change in the system. They not only help a user accomplish a task but also leave an emotional impact about how the process feels. Being intentional about microinteractions can lead to effective visual solutions for interfaces.
